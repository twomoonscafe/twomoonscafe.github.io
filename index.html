About Us

Two Moons Café is a new initiative business based on outer space, where all types of coffee, desserts and appetizers are made. We hope to become a place where you can relax and partake of our extraordinary out of this world treats.

Our vision...
is to inspire and nourish the spirit, a person, cup and galaxy at a time. To offer a sensational experience and a pleasant meeting space for coffee lovers, where they can share unforgettable moments with loved ones, enjoying the variety of delicious products we offer.

We're open seven days a week, from 8:30am-8:00pm for you to enjoy!
